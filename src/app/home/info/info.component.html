 How It Works

MenuMash gets the menu items from local restaurants, shuffles them up, and displays them in a "stack of cards" that is easy to swipe through.


All of the online ordering websites & apps require you to select a category or specific restaurant just to view the items!


With MenuMash you get to quickly filter through everything and order the ones that you liked.

 Disclaimer

All food images are from a data source not affiliated with the restaurants and do not depict the exact menu item, just a similar version.


All personal and app data is kept secure in accordance to the best web standards.
